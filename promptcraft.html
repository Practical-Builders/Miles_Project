About PromptCraft

PromptCraft is an AI prompting school built to help anyone — from curious beginners to working professionals — learn how to communicate effectively with AI.

What we do

We teach prompt engineering through structured lessons, hands-on sandbox practice, and AI-graded missions. Our curriculum is organized into 13 learning paths covering everything from core foundations to advanced domain-specific techniques in writing, code, research, marketing, and more.

Why it matters

The difference between a mediocre AI interaction and a genuinely useful one comes down to how you ask. Prompt engineering is quickly becoming a foundational skill — not just for developers, but for anyone who uses AI tools in their work or daily life. PromptCraft makes that skill learnable, measurable, and fun.

Built with Claude

PromptCraft is powered by Anthropic's Claude AI for all sandbox runs, grading, and feedback. Every score and suggestion you receive comes from a real AI evaluation of your prompt.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

What we collect

When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and a hashed (encrypted) password. If you sign in with Google or Microsoft, we receive your name and email from those providers — we never see your password. We also store your learning progress (XP, completed lessons, passed missions) so it persists across sessions.

How we use it

Your data is used solely to operate PromptCraft — authenticating you, saving your progress, and providing AI sandbox functionality. We do not sell your data, share it with third parties for advertising, or use it for any purpose outside of delivering this service.

Sandbox prompts

Prompts you run in the sandbox are sent to Anthropic's Claude API to generate responses and scores. Please refer to Anthropic's privacy policy for how they handle API data.

Data storage

Your account data is stored in a secure PostgreSQL database hosted on Render. We use industry-standard encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest.

Cookies

We store a JWT authentication token in your browser's localStorage to keep you logged in. We do not use third-party tracking cookies or advertising cookies.

Your rights

You can delete your account at any time from Settings → Data. This permanently removes all your personal data from our systems. For any other privacy requests, contact us at promptcraftadmin@gmail.com.

Changes

We may update this policy as the product evolves. Significant changes will be communicated via email or an in-app notice.
